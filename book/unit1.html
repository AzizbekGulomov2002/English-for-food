IV. Reading Task

Read the passage for overall understanding.

Food is a vital component of our lives. The food industry is the basic need for every country. It was developed by Egyptians and Greeks millions of years ago. Milling and baking were in practice, olive oil and honey were commonly exchanged. Cheese and butter were also historic food.

People started to grow food as an industry when the chemical technology emerged. Sugar manufacture, cane sugar, beet sugar, fermentation, wine making, beer brewing, bread baking, manufacture of vinegar and essential oils were the products of early food technology. Today the food industry covers diverse activities including food supply, production, harvesting, processing, packaging, transportation, distribution, consumption, and disposal.

Food is defined as anything that can be regarded as food, whether it comes from a natural source of meat, eggs, milk, or apple; an integrally processed product like flour and cooked meals for example cakes. But, scientifically speaking, food is not as much a substance that we eat as a substance that provides certain nutrients when we eat. Therefore, nutritionists use the term "stuffs" to refer to these parts of food that a person may eat, in particular proteins, fats and carbohydrates. A person must have enough knowledge of the composition, properties and use of food by human body in order to be a very competent food technology engineer or food technologist.

The food industry is a branch of the national economy that produces food products. The network includes meat and milk, oil and fish products, flour and cereals, pasta, canned fruit and vegetables, milk and oil, sugar, tea packaging, confectionery, bread, grapes and champagne, alcohol, vodka, tobacco, beer, thirst-quenching drinks, soap and other industrial enterprises.

VI. Grammar.Past simple tense.

Positive : Subject + Verb (2nd Form) + Object.
Negative : Subject + did not + Verb (1st Form) + Object.
Question: Did + Subject + Verb (1st Form) + Object?
Positive : Ancient people cultivated harvest as a food source.
Negative : Ancient people didn`t cultivate harvest as a food source. Question " Did ancient people cultivate harvest as a food source?
The Past Simple is used: To express completed action in the past Example: People started to grow food millions of years ago. To describe a series of completed actions in the past Example: The farmer harvested all sugar canes and took them to the juice extraction process. To express habits in the past Example: To be a professional nutritionist, Karim studied lots of books about food properties.
