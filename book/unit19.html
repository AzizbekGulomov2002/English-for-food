Pasta products are semi-finished culinary products prepared from flour and water, sometimes with the addition of protein additives or flavoring substances. Pasta products are widely used in everyday life, general nutrition and production of nutritional concentrates due to their quick cooking (cooking duration is 3-20 minutes depending on the variety), good compatibility with other food products and high nutritional value. Due to its low humidity (13%), it can be stored for more than a year without changing its properties, it is resistant to transportation. In the standard, the following description or standards of the quality indicators of pasta products are given: color, surface, shape, taste, smell, state after boiling, moisture, acidity, consistency, fractures, deformed products and crumbs, the amount of metallomagnetic impurities or absence of pests. The color of the products should be creamy or yellow, uniform, suitable for the type of flour, without traces of unmixed flour, and the color of the product prepared by adding additives should be unique. The shape should match its name. Bends and curves are allowed in pasta, vermicelli and noodles. When boiled until ready, pasta products should not lose their shape, should not stick, should not fall apart. Moisture content of products should not exceed 13%, acidity should not exceed 3%, 10% for tomato products.
