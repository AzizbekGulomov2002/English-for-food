VI. Grammar. Present Simple Passive


Affirmative: Health problems of birds are detected by artificial intelligence.
Negative: Health problems of birds are not detected by artificial intelligence
Question: Are health problems of birds detected by artificial intelligence?
- The passive is more formal than the active and it is more common in written language. We often use the passive when we don’t know who is responsible for the action: Genetically modified organisms are inserted into plant genes.
- We can use “by” to say who or what is responsible for the action: Genetically modified organisms are inserted into plant genes by food scientists. advanced food technology
