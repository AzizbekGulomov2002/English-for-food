IV. Reading task. Read the passage for overall understanding paying attention to the bolded words.
In Uzbekistan, the food industry is supported by several key institutions that oversee its development and regulation. One such institution is the Ministry of Agriculture, which plays a vital role in promoting agricultural growth and ensuring the quality and safety of food products. The Ministry works to implement policies and programs aimed at modernizing the agricultural sector and improving productivity. Another important institution is the State Inspectorate for Control over the Quality and Safety of Food Products (Uzstandart Agency). This agency is responsible for monitoring and regulating food safety standards throughout the country. It conducts inspections, enforces regulations, and certifies food products to ensure they meet quality and safety requirements. Additionally, the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of the Republic of Uzbekistan provides support to businesses in the food industry by facilitating trade, offering business development services, and representing the interests of the sector both domestically and internationally. Furthermore, the Tashkent Institute of Irrigation and Agricultural Mechanization Engineers and other agricultural universities play a significant role in educating and training professionals in fields related to food production, processing, and technology.V.
