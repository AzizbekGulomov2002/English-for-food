IV. Reading task. Read the passage for overall understanding.

The Republic of Uzbekistan has a modern food industry that occupies an important position in the national economy. This industry is mainly based on the processing of local raw materials. The food industry accounts for 8.1% of the country's total industrial output. The network includes meat-milk, fat-oil, fish products, flour and cereals, bread, pasta, fruit and vegetable preserves, packaging, grape and champagne wine, alcohol, vodka, tobacco, beer, thirst- quenching drinks, soap and other industries. New modern technologies and equipment are being installed in enterprises. Measures are being taken to meet the needs of the population with food products produced in the country itself. Because the contribution of food products in the export is the largest. One of the leading sectors of the republic's food industry is oil production. Since ancient times vegetable oil, sesame, flax, cottonseed, and linseed have been obtained from seeds. This food industry accounts for 25% of the total production volume. The first cotton seed oil factory in Uzbekistan was built in 1884 in Kokan. Currently, there are large oil extraction factories in Kasansay, Gulistan, Ferghana, Kokan, Kattakorghan, Denov and other cities.

VII. Classroom activity. “GUESS WHAT” METHOD

Divide the students into three groups. Elicit the cards and give them 2 minutes to prepare to speak. A member from each group explains step by step cooking process of a given meal. The other group should find which meal is being cooked. Winner is the one who makes less mistakes and uses more phrasal verbs.
