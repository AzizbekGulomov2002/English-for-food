Track 59. Listen to the passage in reading task.

Global food industry institutions play pivotal roles in ensuring food security, safety, and sustainability worldwide. Among these institutions, the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) stands out as a leading entity within the United Nations system, working to eliminate hunger and improve nutrition. Through research, policy development, and technical assistance, the FAO addresses issues such as food production, distribution, and agricultural development on a global scale.Additionally, the World Health Organization (WHO) plays a crucial role in safeguarding public health related to food consumption. It sets international standards and guidelines for food safety and nutrition, collaborating with governments and other stakeholders to promote healthy diets and prevent foodborne illnesses. On the trade front, the World Trade Organization (WTO) oversees international trade agreements and negotiations concerning agricultural products and food commodities. It aims to facilitate fair and transparent trade practices while addressing trade barriers and disputes that may affect the global food supply chain. international food organizations Furthermore, organizations like the Codex Alimentarius Commission establish standards and guidelines for food safety and quality, providing a framework for international trade and consumer protection.

VIII. Classroom activity. Word combos
Ask students to come to take cards turned back and sit according to different words combining with other words. Then write several sentences with gaps to fill out with quantifiers. Students are asked to attach the appropriate quantifier in the gaps. At the end of the activity motivate them to make their own sentences for the quantifier they have taken.
